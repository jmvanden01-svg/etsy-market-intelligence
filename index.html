Etsy Market Intelligence Tool

Market Research Platform for Makers & 3D Printing Businesses

Status: Currently in development for internal use

About This Project

The Etsy Market Intelligence Tool is a data analysis platform designed to help makers and 3D printing businesses make informed product decisions by analyzing marketplace trends and consumer behavior on Etsy.

Purpose

This tool analyzes publicly available Etsy marketplace data to identify trending products, popular search terms, and emerging opportunities in the maker space. By providing data-driven insights, we help small businesses optimize their product development efforts and reduce the opportunity cost of creating items with limited market appeal.

Key Features

  • Trend analysis across 3D printed product categories
  • Search term and keyword tracking
  • Price point and competitive analysis
  • Sales velocity indicators for emerging products
  • Weekly market intelligence reports

Intended Use

This tool is being developed for use by our 3D printing business to improve product selection and reduce design time spent on items with limited market demand. We analyze public Etsy data in accordance with Etsy's Terms of Service and API guidelines to generate market insights that inform our business decisions.

All data collection and analysis respects marketplace guidelines and focuses on publicly available information to help small businesses compete more effectively in the handmade and custom manufacturing space.
